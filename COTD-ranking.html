How does this leaderboard works?

Depending on the number x you'll choose below, it'll calculate the average position of all the players on their last x COTDs (if they played that many at least), and rank the players accordingly.

Ranks are based on the relative position in % as this allows to take into account the variation of the total number of players per cotd (which varies from day to day ^-^) (for exemple: someone who placed 500th out of 1000 player (top 50%) has actually performed worst than someone else who would have finished 600th out of 1500 players (top 40%))
